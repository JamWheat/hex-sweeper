How to Play

The object of Hex Sweeper is to open all off the empty cells without hitting any mines. Hitting just one mine ends the game!

Left click on a cell to open it.

The number on an empty cell tells you how many mines are adjecent to that cell.
Use that information to figure out where the mines are.
Right click on a closed cell to put a flag on it
